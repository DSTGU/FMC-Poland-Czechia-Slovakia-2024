### Pending registrations
On this list there are competitors who have registered but have not yet paid the entry fee. Registration requirements specify that registrations will be accepted in order of submitting registrations, not paying, so people on this list have a spot reserved for them and count towards venue competitor limit even before their entry fee is paid
